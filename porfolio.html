Orchestrate

A web application that allows classical musicians both professional and freelance to book venues and rehearsal spaces as well as to socialise and interact with other musicians within the application. Some of the technologies used include Mongo DB, mongoose, socket.io and passport JS. The app was created with four team members. We used agile practices such as daily scrums and mob/ pair programming. Thanks be to Benjamin Thomas for the logo design and help with video editing!
